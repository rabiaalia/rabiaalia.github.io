Have a question? Check out the FAQ

From canvas to community, we've got answers. Find out more about Rabia Alia's art, impact, and journey below.

What kind of art does Rabia Alia create?

Rabia Alia is a visual artist who uniquely blends traditional calligraphy with abstract strokes and digital mapping. Her work creates a harmonious fusion of cultural heritage and modern artistic expression.

Does Rabia Alia accept commission work?

Yes, Rabia Alia does accept commission work. Please reach out through the contact form on this website to discuss your project.

What are some of Rabia Alia's key achievements?

Rabia has received numerous accolades, including the Youth Icon Award of the Year 2024 and multiple national-level calligraphy competition wins. She also led her team to win 1st Prize in the Spring Fest 2023 Mural Competition.

Is Rabia Alia involved in social activism or community work?

Absolutely. Beyond her artistic endeavors, Rabia is a dedicated social activist and art leader. She holds key leadership roles in national and international organizations, focusing on youth empowerment and promoting art culture.

Has Rabia Alia's work been exhibited?

Yes, Rabia has participated in group show exhibitions in various art galleries. Her work was recently showcased at a calligraphy exhibition at Coopera Art Gallery, Lahore, on April 19, 2025.

What is "Cartographic Quest"?

"Cartographic Quest" is a unique cartographic artwork by Rabia Alia that captures the essence of sacred inscriptions, place, and God’s love. It merges the dynamic beauty of hand lettering with the precision of digital mapping.

How can I connect with Rabia Alia?

You can connect with Rabia through the contact form on this website, or by reaching out via her linked social media profiles, such as LinkedIn.
